About Kayceclose

Elliman Florida agent Kayce Driscoll has over a decade of experience in the Real Estate Industry, specializing in luxury waterfront properties. Luxury Condos "on the sand" and homes, specifically on Hibiscus, Palm, Star, Sunsets and the Venetian Islands in Miami Beach.

Prior to joining Elliman Florida, Kayce worked with a boutique firm, during which time she won numerous awards including Top Producer, International Top Producer, and the Best of the Beach Award. In fact, she sold two commercial deals, each over $1M, her first week in the business. Kayce has her proverbial finger on the pulse of the market. "I can feel the market turning, because I am out there every day," she says. "I am not just a Realtor but an investor, too, so I watch out for my clients the same way I would watch out for my own properties." She considers herself a natural salesperson, and has a talent for listening to her clients' "wish lists" and locating the perfect property in 3 days.

Kayce's success in sales has been proven in multiple industries, as she managed a 450 person sales organization at TDS prior to the age of 40. As technology sector emerged, she became the top producer and won awards as MVP at Oracle Corporation, where her team sold a $136M software deal.

When she is not working, Kayce loves to travel, having visited over 50 countries. She also enjoys skiing while at her second home in Aspen, art, animal rescue, the water, ballet and entertaining.

Liz takes a personalized real estate approach with each of her clients, focusing on their specific buying or selling needs.
